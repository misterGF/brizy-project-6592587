About Us

We are a global team of innovators. We believe that businesses of all sizes should be able to leverage technology. At AmplifySMS, we are tackling customer engagement issues that most businesses face.

Our overarching goal is to bridge the digital divide between major corporations and our beloved small businesses. As technology becomes more and more integrated, we are here to help level the playing field. Our business is to make yours succeed.

Visit our parent company ByteSize Labs, to learn more.

Our headquarter is in Boston, Mass

Our journey started in our neighborhood, Dorchester. This is where we saw the need and worked with businesses to develop our solution. Now our team has grown globally. Our team members can be found in Brazil, Azerbaijan, Kenya & the Philippines.
