Frequently Asked Questions

The most common questions about how our platform works.

How does it work?

We provide our customers a website portal to manage their text messages. Our platform is easy to use. It feels similar to the messaging apps you use on your phone. The only difference is that you will be texting as your business.

Can I send images?

Of course! Our guidance recommends that you use images to personalize your messages. They tend to be more intimate and effective in converting customers.

Can I use this for appointment reminders?

Soon you will be able to configure appointment reminder messages. All messages can be scheduled to be sent at a specific time and day.

Can I receive voice calls?

Yes, the numbers you have with us can receive voice calls. You have the choice of forwarding all calls to a number of your choice or playing a custom message.
